📋 Kaise Use Karein — 3 Simple Steps

  1. Print karein — A4 size, color print (ek sheet mein 2 cards)
  2. Laminate karwao — ₹5-10 mein local dukan pe milti hai lamination
  3. Fold karein dashed line pe — tent shape bana jaayega, table pe rakhein
💡 Pro Tip: Har table pe ek card rakho + counter pe ek bada card lagao. Customers khud scan karenge — aapko kuch nahi kehna padega! Paytm wale ki tarah natural lagega.
